Getting to Evanston:

From Midway and O'Hare airports, your options include taxi and the L (Chicago's elevated train system run by the Chicago Transit Authority [CTA]).

  • A taxi will cost around $40 from O'Hare and around $60 from Midway. You can call Norshore Cab at 847-864-7500 to arrange a somewhat less expensive fixed-rate taxi ride between O'Hare and Evanston.
  • The L costs $2.25 and will take you approximately 90 minutes from either airport. The Davis St. station is the closest station to the Orrington Hotel (see the map). From O'Hare, take the blue line to Jackson, transfer to the red line towards Howard, at Howard take the purple line towards Linden, get off at Davis. From Midway, take the orange line to Roosevelt, transfer to the red line towards Howard, at Howard take the purple line towards Linden, get off at Davis. For more information, visit the CTA website.
