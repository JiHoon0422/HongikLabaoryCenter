Complexity Conference


March 6-7, 2011

Northwestern University, Evanston, IL


NICO (Northwestern Institute on Complex Systems) and SONIC (Science of Networks in Communities Research Group at Northwestern) cordially invite you to the 2011 Complexity Conference.

 

The purpose of this conference is to bring together leading researchers and present the most exciting interdisciplinary research in complex systems and network science.

 

In conjunction with the main conference, a Complexity Tutorial will be held on March 5.
